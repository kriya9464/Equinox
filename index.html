Welcome to Conjunctions'23

Conjunctions 2023 is the ultimate platform for those who want to showcase their creative ideas, their passion for technology and their commitment to preserving our shared values. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or a curious novice, this hackathon is the perfect opportunity to let your talents shine and create solutions that can make a real difference in the world.

At Celestial Biscuit IGDTUW, we believe that technology can be a powerful force for good, and that by working together, we can harness its potential to create a brighter future for all. That's why we've created Conjunctions 2023, a hackathon that combines the best of technology, creativity and social impact.

So if you're looking for a challenge that will push you to your limits, and a community that will inspire you to achieve great things, then Conjunctions 2023 is the event for you. Join us and let's create something amazing together!
